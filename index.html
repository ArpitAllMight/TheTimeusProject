Inspiration

There is something almost poetic about it.
It doesn't come to you,
You have to reach out to it.
You have to show commitment towards it.
And that commitment comes through movement.
Because it is movement that ensures…

That you are
in the right place
at the right time.

That helps
you find
your move.

That enables you to

leave a mark

on the universe.

That let's you
groove to
your tune.

And that makes
your dreams
come true.

Because in the end,
it is Movement that Inspires us all.
